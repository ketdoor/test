About us

Dar Al Anmar Engineering General Contracting Company (Limited Liability Company) was founded in 2008 and has since specialized in the fields of construction, paving, paving and maintenance of roads and building construction. The company has a team of experienced and efficient engineers and technicians, enabling it to carry out various projects with high efficiency. The company carries out all kinds of road and bridge projects, as well as residential city projects and we are committed to providing high quality services according to the highest engineering standards, based on the latest technology and methods in the industry. We believe in the importance of sustainability and social responsibility, so we are keen to apply green building principles and environmentally friendly technologies in all our projects. Our goal is to achieve custom-er satisfaction by providing innovative solutions that meet their needs and exceed their expectations. With our commit-ment to quality and innovation, Dar Al Anmar Engineering remains a reliable partner in the construction and contracting industry, committed to continuing excellence and leadership in providing the best engineering solutions.
