Our Project

Project Name:

Construction of Plant Entrance Methods - Quarries

Project Location:

Taji - Baghdad/Iraq

contract Term:

100 days

Contract number & value:

$ 8,000,000

Date of commencement of the contract:

01 October 2022

Details and role of Dlo Group and Dar Al Anmar Engineering Company during the implementation of this project:

Due to the excellent expertise and possession of a group of machinery and manpower on which the two companies were nominated to carry out the work of processing concretate concrete and casting using a Farsha Concreet device for pouring squares and streets, the design is done by pouring Round Street and Yap with a total width of 8.5m and 30cm thickness and length (15) km with an estimated amount of 127,500m2 and a compression force of 35 MicaPascal for a life of 28 days. All joints contain 20 mm thickness trades, concreet joint work and stretch joint treatment. All work paragraphs have been implemented according to the approved plans and specifications and by international standards.
